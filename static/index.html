License your Linked Data

Licentia is a web service to help in the search of a suitable license for works within the Linked Data initiative.

Find the perfect license

Use our application to find the perfect license for your work. You only have to mark what permissions, requirements and prohibitions a user will be able to do with your work. Our app will give the licenses that match your criteria.

Download the license RDF

Once you find the license your are looking, you will be able to download it in RDF Turtle format to use it for your needs. You are linked to the license human readable specification as well to check out the license on your own.
